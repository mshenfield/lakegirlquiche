The Launch of Lake Girl Quiche

I started making quiches as a thank you to my friends and family for the kindness and love I have received from them. The business evolved as a direct result of their encouragement.

The creative work behind Lake Girl Quiche was a collaboration by some of my favorite kids I used to whip on a tube on the back of my boat. In particular - Max and Morgan! As well as my dear friend Eric without whose support Lake Girl Quiche wouldn't have taken off.

Now I get to share it with you and spread that love and joy. Sit back, relax and make more great memories for those you care for.

C'est la vie
